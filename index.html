Bookkeeping FAQs

Answering your questions

What is your pricing structure?

My pricing varies depending on the services required and the complexity of the work. I offer hourly, daily, monthly, tailored bundles and project-based rates. I'm happy to provide a free consultation to discuss your needs and provide a personalized quote.

What type of businesses do you work with?

I work with a diverse clientele, including small businesses, startups, and individuals, both locally in Gadsden, AL and surrounding areas, and remotely across a wider region. My bookkeeping services are tailored to meet the unique needs of your industry, whether you are based right here or operate from afar.

What accounting software do you work with?

I primarily work with QuickBooks Online and Xero accounting software. Software choice can depend on your needs and service plans available. Importantly, I can adapt to your existing software if preferred and assist with migrating to QuickBooks or Xero if you wish to switch.

Do you offer mobile notary services?

Yes, absolutely! I provide a full range of notary services, including in-person notarizations at agreed locations, online notarizations for remote convenience, and mobile notary services where I travel to your location within Etowah County. Read more about my notary services.
