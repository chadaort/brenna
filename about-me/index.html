I’m Brenna—a proud Texan at heart who’s found a home in Gadsden, Alabama. For over 15 years, I’ve been helping small businesses like yours navigate the world of bookkeeping. My journey started in home development and construction accounting, but my passion has always been about one thing: helping small businesses thrive.

I truly enjoy being a bookkeeper—bringing peace of mind and a little extra clarity to hardworking business owners, so they can focus on growing their dreams with confidence in their numbers!

I know how overwhelming it can feel to juggle the financial side of things while trying to run your business, serve your customers, and chase your dreams. That’s where I come in. I’m here to simplify your finances, keep your books organized, and give you the clarity and confidence you need to succeed. Whether you’re in construction, retail, or any other industry, I’ve got the tools and experience to make your life easier.

I’ve worked with QuickBooks, JD Edwards, and Trueline, and I’m no stranger to advanced Excel and Office skills. From tax prep (think 1099 forms and payroll) to reporting for tax professionals, I handle it all. And as a Notary Public in Alabama (and formerly in two other states), I’m here to support your document needs too.

When I’m not knee-deep in spreadsheets, you’ll find me at home with my wonderful spouse and our five lively dogs (yes, five—it’s a full house!). We love cooking up Tex-Mex dishes and smoking meat on the pit—because nothing brings people together like great food and good company. We’re also big fans of exploring our Gadsden community, whether it’s discovering amazing local food or listening to live music. Every place has something special to offer, and we’re always excited to find new favorites.

At the end of the day, I believe in doing work that matters—helping small businesses like yours succeed, one balanced ledger at a time. Let’s simplify your finances so you can focus on what you do best: running your business, serving your community, and living your life to the fullest.

Reach out today, and let’s get started. I can’t wait to meet you!

Warmly, Brenna
