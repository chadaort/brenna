How to prepare for your notary appointment

A little preparation goes a long way. Before we meet, make sure you have the correct documents ready for notarization and review them for accuracy. Bring a valid, government-issued photo ID to verify your identity, and hold off on signing anything beforehand—most notarial acts require signatures to be witnessed.
