Essential bookkeeping services

A well-organized general ledger is the backbone of your business's finances. It’s not just about balancing numbers—it’s about understanding where you stand and making decisions that drive growth.

Here’s how I help keep your general ledger in check…

I’ll dive into your records, looking for any discrepancies or misplaced entries. Then, I’ll reconcile everything to make sure your books match up with your bank statements, all while keeping everything neat and tidy. This not only eliminates errors but gives you a crystal-clear view of your financial health.

Managing your payables and receivables is like keeping your business’s heartbeat steady. It helps make sure bills are paid on time and that you’re getting paid for your hard work.

How I take care of AP and AR for you…

I stay on top of the bills you owe and the money that’s owed to you. I’ll follow up with vendors and customers, making sure everything stays in motion without the stress. The goal is to keep your cash flow steady and your financial worries at bay.

Your financial records should reflect reality, and reconciliation ensures everything lines up as it should. This isn’t just about checking numbers—it’s about catching discrepancies before they cause bigger issues.

How I handle reconciliation…

I’ll carefully go over every transaction, comparing your books to your bank and credit card statements. If something doesn’t match, I’ll sort it out, ensuring your records stay accurate and free from errors. It’s all about peace of mind and staying in control of your finances.

Tracking expenses is about more than just keeping receipts—it’s about understanding where your money is going and finding opportunities for savings and growth. By organizing and categorizing your records, I help streamline tax reporting, uncover cost-saving opportunities, and provide insights for smarter decisions—so you can focus on growing your profitability.

Here’s how I track your expenses…

I’ll organize all your receipts, invoices, and transactions into clear categories that make sense for your business. Each expense gets recorded in real time, giving you insights into spending patterns, helping you make smarter financial choices, and ensuring you’re set up to save on taxes when it counts.

Financial reports aren’t just about numbers—they’re about telling the story of your business. They give you a clear view of your financial health, showing income, expenses, and profitability. More than that, they help you track performance, make informed decisions, and ensure tax compliance. By understanding where you’ve been, these reports become a roadmap for where you’re going, supporting your business’s growth and long-term success.

How I create your financial reports…

I’ll put together easy-to-understand reports that speak to your business’s performance—whether it’s income statements, balance sheets, or cash flow reports. These reports aren’t just for tax season; they’re tools you can use year-round to track your progress and make informed decisions.

Payroll is one of those things that’s easy to overlook until it’s not right. But getting it right is crucial to keeping your team happy and your business compliant.

How I manage payroll for you…

I’ll set up systems that ensure your employees are paid accurately, on time, and with all deductions handled properly. I take the stress out of payroll, saving you time and keeping everything running smoothly so you can focus on what matters—your team and your business’s growth.
